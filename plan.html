Goal

The goal of the 100SMILES Dental Care website is to attract new patients and make it easier for people to book appointments online. The site will promote the clinic’s services, experienced staff, and first-visit discounts to increase local awareness and bookings.

Success Evaluation

  • Increase in online appointment enquiries/bookings compared to current average.
  • More visits to Contact and Services pages (higher engagement).
  • Positive feedback from patients about ease of navigation and information access.

Target Audience

The main target audience is local families and working adults in Razole who want reliable and friendly dental care. The website will use a clean layout, simple navigation, readable headings, and clear “Book via Email” and “Call Now” buttons so all users can quickly find services, staff details, and contact options without confusion.

Site Flowchart

The site includes five main pages: Home, Services, Staff, Articles, and Contact. The navigation bar appears on every page for easy access.

Site navigation structure showing flow from Home → Services, Staff, Articles → Contact.

WireFrames

Home: Header & navigation, hero banner with welcome message, boxes for Our Services / About the Clinic / Contact, and footer with address & opening hours.
Services: Preventive Care, Restorative/Surgery, Orthodontics/Cosmetic, plus a side box for first-visit discount and 50+ years’ experience.
Contact: Phone, Email, Address/Map, a “Book via Email” button, and Opening Hours footer.
